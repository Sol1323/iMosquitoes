Mosquito-borne viruses are also a problem: Over 100 million people are infected with Dengue virus in the tropics and subtropics every year. Recently Chikungunya and Zika virus have emerged and spread. West Nile virus reached N. America in 1999 and has spread. Although once thought stamped out in much of the N.
